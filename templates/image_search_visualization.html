How Our Image Search Works

Technology Behind Image Search

Our image search leverages cutting-edge computer vision to match your uploaded images with visually similar products in our database.

Text Search vs. Image Search

Text Search Image Search
Requires exact keywords Upload an image, no words needed
Limited to descriptions Matches based on visual features
"red lipstick" Image of a red lipstick tube
May miss visual matches Finds look-alikes directly

Technology Stack

  • MobileNetV2: Efficient CNN for feature extraction
  • Feature Vectors: 1,280-dimensional image representations
  • Cosine Similarity: Measuring visual similarity
  • Transfer Learning: Pre-trained model optimization
  • Ranking: Top visual matches prioritized

MobileNetV2 Feature Extraction

MobileNetV2 Architecture

MobileNetV2 processes images through multiple convolutional layers to extract visual features.

Feature Detection Process

MobileNetV2 processes images through:

  • Early Layers: Detect edges and colors
  • Middle Layers: Identify textures and patterns
  • Deep Layers: Recognize shapes and objects
  • Final Output: Compact 1,280-dimensional feature vector
